# Content

This site exists to show work Sam Young has genuinely shipped. Trust is more
valuable than polish.

## Content rules

- Do not invent or embellish projects, roles, clients, collaborators, dates,
  outcomes, metrics, testimonials, or awards.
- Distinguish clearly between shipped work, experiments, and work in progress.
- Use specific evidence when it is available: the problem, Sam's contribution,
  what shipped, and the observable result.
- If a fact is missing, leave a visible placeholder or ask Sam. Do not infer a
  flattering answer.
- Do not publish confidential project details, private contact information, or
  third-party assets without permission.
- Keep voice direct and human. Avoid inflated agency language and generic AI
  copy.

## Project entry minimum

A published project should have:

- an accurate title and short description;
- Sam's role or contribution;
- a truthful shipped-status label;
- suitable imagery with meaningful alternative text when needed; and
- a working destination or an honest explanation when the work is not public.

Detailed project content can move into dedicated files when real entries are
added. This document remains the policy source rather than a content database.
